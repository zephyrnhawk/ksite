    Kelsey Martin (aka bopx) 1992, CA

    I'm currently a student studying Visual Development as a Game Design major at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco. I communicate well with others and I'm always pushing myself (along with my peers) to keep learning, improving, and growing. As I'm still developing a focus, I continuously develop my skills in every area of design as I try to not limit myself as a concept artist.

    While I've always loved drawing, my deepest passion has been in gaming. I love a game's visual as well as non-visual design, and the wonderful relationship the two create. Out of studying, I enjoy playing games, making props, making costumes, and shooting my friends with paint.
